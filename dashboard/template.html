Pipeline Architecture

Two-Stage Churn Strategy
Pre-Activation model + Retention model ‚Üí combined risk score

Never-Activated Risk
Predicts whether a newly-won seller will fail to place their first order within 90 days. Features: lead behaviour, business segment, sales cycle duration.
Stage 2 ¬∑ Retention
Dormancy Risk
Identifies active sellers at risk of going dormant (60+ days idle). Features: GMV trend, order frequency, days since last sale, customer diversity.
Combined Score
